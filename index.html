📖 How to Send a Connection Request Manually

1
Copy your personalised message
Click the button below to copy the message to your clipboard. It's personalised for this executive.
✓ Copied!
2
Open their LinkedIn profile
Click the button to open their profile in a new tab. Their direct LinkedIn URL is pre-loaded.
3
Click "Connect" on their profile
On their profile page, click the blue Connect button (or the More → Connect dropdown). LinkedIn will show a dialog.
4
Click "Add a note" and paste your message
In the connection dialog, click Add a note, then paste the message you copied in Step 1 (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V). Keep it under 300 characters.
5
Click Send & mark as done
Click Send in LinkedIn. Then come back here and mark this executive as sent.
⚠️ LinkedIn limit reminder: Send max 20–25 invites per day to keep your account safe. The limits bar at the top tracks your usage.
